Return Policy

Our Commitment to You

At Fretio, we are dedicated to providing a seamless and satisfactory experience for our users. Please review our return policy below:

  • Once an order has been collected by the customer, returns are not permissible.
  • For any inquiries or concerns regarding a product, we encourage you to reach out directly to the seller.
  • Fretio serves as a platform facilitating connections between buyers and sellers and does not assume responsibility for any disputes or issues that may arise from transactions.

Platform Role

As a peer-to-peer marketplace, Fretio acts solely as an intermediary platform that connects campus community members for buying and selling goods. We do not:

  • Take possession of items listed on our platform
  • Verify the condition of items exchanged
  • Guarantee the quality or authenticity of products
  • Process returns between users

All transactions are conducted directly between users, and any arrangements regarding returns should be negotiated between the involved parties.

Recommendations for Buyers

To ensure a satisfactory transaction experience, we recommend the following practices:

  • Thoroughly examine product descriptions, images, and details before completing a purchase
  • Communicate clearly with sellers about your expectations and any questions you may have
  • Inspect items at the time of collection before finalizing the transaction
  • Consider arranging to meet in public places for item exchanges
  • Use the Fretio chat feature to keep all communications on the platform for reference

Reporting Issues

While Fretio does not process returns, we take the safety and satisfaction of our community seriously. If you encounter any of the following situations, please report them to our support team:

  • Fraudulent activity or misrepresentation of items
  • Safety concerns during transactions
  • Harassment or inappropriate behavior from other users
  • Technical issues with the platform affecting your transaction

Our team will review each report and take appropriate action according to our community guidelines and terms of service.
